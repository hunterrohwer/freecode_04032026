# Rally Game Spec

## Core fantasy
Drive a sketchy rally car that feels unstable, dangerous, and hard to control.

## Platform
Mobile, portrait orientation.

## Camera
Third-person camera behind the car.

## Controls
Two thumbs only.
Left thumb controls left-side throttle/brake input.
Right thumb controls right-side throttle/brake input.

## Design goals
- Car should feel fast, unstable, and heavy
- Full throttle should usually be a mistake
- Small mistakes should compound
- Player should feel "I know why I crashed"
- Difficulty should come from car physics plus harder rally stages

## Version 1 scope
- One car
- One test track
- One follow camera
- One reset system
- Simple timer or checkpoint loop
- Portrait mobile control layout
